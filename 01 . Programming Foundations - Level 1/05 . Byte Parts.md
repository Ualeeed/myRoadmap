### There's three part of Byte : 

1. <span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 80)">Bit</span> : is the smallest box with 0 or 1 
2. <span style="color:rgb(255, 255, 0)">crumb</span> : is group of two Bits together 
3. <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Nibble</span> : is a group of 4 Bits together 

|  0  |  0  |  0  |  0  |  1  |  1  |  1  |  1  |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
 - 0000 last 4 bits in 1 Byte called : <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Upper Nibble</span>.
 - 1111 First 4 bits in 1 Byte called : <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Lower Nibble</span>.

- 1 Byte = 8 bits
- 1 Byte = 4 Crumbs 
- 1 Byte = 2 Nibbles
- 1 Nibble = 2 Crumbs
- 1 Nibble = 4 bits
- 1 crumb = 2 bits

By using <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Nibble</span> we can convert hexadecimal to Binary directly.

Example : 

- 1D5 TO Binary : 

  5 → 0101 
  D → 13 → 1101
  1 → 0001
  1D5 = 0001 1101 0101 

- 5E9 TO Binary : 

  9 → 1001
  E → 14 → 1110
  5 → 0101
  5E9 = 0101 1110 1001


By using <span style="color:rgb(255, 192, 0)">Nibble</span> we can convert Binary to Hexadecimal directly.

- 000111010101 to Hexadecimal : 

  0001 = 1
  1101 = 13 = D
  0101 = 5 
  000111010101 = 1D5
