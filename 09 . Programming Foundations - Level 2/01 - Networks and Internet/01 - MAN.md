## ğŸ¯ Key Characteristics of MAN

### **Coverage Area**
- Spans an entire city or large geographical region
- Typical range: 10-50 kilometers
- Can connect multiple neighborhoods, districts, or suburbs

### **Ownership Structure**
- Usually owned by **ISPs** (Internet Service Providers)
- Telecom companies and large corporations
- Sometimes government entities (smart city initiatives)
- Organizations with multiple city locations

### **Technology & Infrastructure**
- **Fiber Optic Cables**: Primary backbone for high-speed data
- **DSL Lines**: Digital Subscriber Line connections
- **Wireless Links**: Microwave and radio frequency connections
- **Cable Networks**: Coaxial cable infrastructure

### **Primary Purpose**
- Connect multiple LANs within the same metropolitan area
- Provide high-speed internet access across a city
- Enable communication between different branches of organizations
- Support smart city infrastructure (traffic systems, public Wi-Fi)
